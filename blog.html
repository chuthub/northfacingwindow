Introduction

25 September 2025

Welcome to Pho-Blog-Graphy. This is intended to be a place where anyone who loves photography can write about any aspect of it that they want to share with the world. I am the moderator of this site, my name is Craig. Please be patient with me while the site is still being built. We will have a submission form for anyone who wants to share, but we're not quite there yet. Keep up with this blog to see how things are shaping up. Also, please be considerate and approproate when sharing on the site. There's no harm in disagreements, just have them respectfully and then we can all learn from each other. Enjoy, and have a great time on the site!
